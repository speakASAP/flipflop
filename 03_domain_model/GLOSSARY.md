## Terms

- Checkout-to-first-revenue: the owner’s near-term business outcome of a production checkout path that can create real revenue safely.
- Provider verification: evidence from a payment provider initiation, callback, webhook, or approved manual verification path.
- Owner-bypassed risk: a known blocker the owner explicitly deferred without marking it verified.
- Approval-first AI content: generated SEO or product content stored as draft until reviewed.
- Intent bundle: the goal, constraints, non-goals, acceptance criteria, validation criteria, and upstream links given to an agent.
